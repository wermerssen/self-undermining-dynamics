Self‑Undermining Dynamics

A Structural Model of Open Political Systems

Overview

Open political systems evolve through recursive feedback loops that generate both stability and instability. This project outlines how structural dynamics can lead to self‑undermining trajectories, where the system’s own mechanisms gradually erode its long‑term resilience.

The model focuses on structural recursion, systemic amplification, and meta‑level transitions that reshape political behavior over time.
